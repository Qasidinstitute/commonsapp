بشوفـ(ك) على خير

بشوفـ(ك) على خير

Meaning: Literally means “see you in a good situation” or in “good times”. It is said when you say goodbye to someone and hope to see them again.

عمر(ي) ما

عمر(ي) ما

Meaning: The phrase is expressed “I never”. Together the expression in the Second phrase expresses “I never did this”. It is said when you want to emphasize something you or somebody else never did or experienced.

أول على/عن آخر

أول على/عن آخر

Meaning: This is a simple phrase literally translated as “first to last”. The phrase is used as to simply express the word “eventually”.

بالي مشغول

بالي مشغول

Meaning: The phrase is used when you want to say “I’m worried about…” or “I am preoccupied with…”. It is primarily used when you want to show concern for somebody or something. It is also used when you hear bad news about family or friends or want to find out why someone was absent from a situation or event. It could also be used when asking or inquiring about a new experience someone is having in his/her life.

واخيرا فرجت

واخيرا فرجت

Meaning: The phrase is primarily used to express a problem or issue that has finally been solved or over. It is used when someone has experienced suffering or hardship and that suffering or hardship has finally ended.
